# ✅ Key KPIs

* **Total Sales:** Overall revenue generated from all products sold.
* **Average Sales:** Average revenue generated per transaction.
* **Number of Items:** Total number of items available for sale.
* **Average Rating:** Average customer rating across all products.
* **Low Fat Sales:** Revenue generated from Low Fat products.

# 📈 Insights Generated

## 🔹 Top Performing Product Categories

Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods generated the highest sales revenue, indicating strong customer demand for everyday grocery products.

## 🔹 Low Fat Products Dominate Sales

Low Fat products contributed approximately ₹776K in revenue, significantly outperforming Regular products and highlighting consumer preference for healthier food options.

## 🔹 Tier 3 Outlets Lead Revenue Generation

Tier 3 outlets generated the highest sales revenue, making them the best-performing outlet category across all tiers.

## 🔹 Peak Sales Performance in 2018

Outlets established in 2018 recorded the highest sales performance, contributing approximately ₹0.20M in revenue.

## 🔹 Medium-Sized Outlets Perform Best

Medium-sized outlets contributed the largest share of revenue compared to Small and High-sized outlets.

## 🔹 Strong Customer Satisfaction

Average customer ratings remained consistently high at 4.0, indicating positive customer experiences across products.

## 🔹 Consistent Outlet Performance

All outlet tiers contributed significantly to total sales, with Tier 3 outlets showing the strongest overall performance.
